Accounting

A Tally and Tally Prime course provides practical training in using Tally software for accounting, financial management, and GST compliance. The course covers key features like ledger management, inventory tracking, invoicing, and financial reporting. Students learn to streamline accounting processes, generate reports, and understand taxation.

Course Content and Skills:

  • Accounting Fundamentals:Understanding the accounting cycle, financial statements, and ledger maintenance.
  • Tally Prime Features:Creating and managing ledgers, recording transactions (vouchers), and generating financial reports.
  • Inventory Management:Tracking inventory, managing stock, and recording transactions.
  • Taxation (GST):Understanding GST concepts, recording GST transactions, and filing GST returns.
  • Financial Reporting:Creating and analyzing financial reports, including profit & loss, balance sheets, and cash flow statements.
  • Banking and Payments:Managing bank accounts, recording bank transactions, and generating bank reconciliation reports.
  • Payroll and TDS:Managing payroll, calculating and deducting TDS, and filing relevant returns.
  • Customization and Advanced Features:Learning to customize Tally for specific business needs and utilizing advanced features.

Course Duration:

The students are to practice an hour a day for 2 months for the completion of the course.

Eligibility:

There are no specific eligibility criteria for this course, making it accessible to anyone interested in learning about computers.

Classes are conducted from 9am to 5pm on weekdays. Apart from this, students will have access to course materials and support throughout the duration.

Certification:

Upon successful completion of the course, participants receive a certificate that is recognized by various government and private organizations.
